Team Boron

Team Boron is a global collaboration of brilliant scientists with various backgrounds and specialities in biology, chemistry and engineering. Our team is composed of researchers from Toronto Metropolitan University, Mt. Allison University, Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology, Baylor University, and Virginia Tech.
